About

Here is a little something to get to know me.

  • 1976-1999

    My Humble Beginnings

    I was born and raised in Italy. My first employer was my Grandpa, a WW2 veteran. He had a huge role creating the extremely professional individual I am today, with an impeccable work ethic and loyalty. I met my husband while he was stationed in Italy with the Navy. We got married in 1998.

  • November 1999

    I moved to Wyoming

    Once my husband's tour overseas was over, we moved to Cheyenne Wy, to be closer to his parents. Here we started our own little family. We have two boys, Jordan 18, and Dylan 14.

  • May 2000 - June 2017

    I started my job at a local outdoor retailer.

    I started from the bottom and worked my way up. I was promoted to the marketing department as the Catalog Production Assistant. A few years later I was promoted to Catalog Designer and was assigned to the Home & Gift Catalog. After obtaining my Web Designer Certificate, I was promoted to Visual Web Designer. I worked on the company's email marketing campaigns, maintained store portals, led marketing campaigns, I updated and maintained various landing pages.

  • June 2017

    I attended Array School of Technology and Design

    In January 2017 the company went through restructure and decided to do without their local marketing department, the whole department was laid off. I had always been very passionate about code and development. As a Visual Web Designer I have noticed a big disconnect between Designers and Developers. Before looking for another job, I decided to attend a coding school. I want to sharpen some old skills, gain some new ones, and after graduation, I want to get back to the job market as a Front End Web Developer and bridge that gap.

  • Stay Tuned
    for More to Come!
